About Our College

Welcome To BIET Davangere

The Bapuji Educational Association (BEA), BIET - An Autonomous Institute affiliated to VTU-Belgavi approved by AICTE,New Delhi. , founded in 1958 in Davangere, is one of Karnataka’s oldest and most respected educational institutions, created to serve students aspiring for higher education.

From a single first-grade college, BEA has expanded into a vast educational network offering programs from Nursery to Postgraduate, Engineering, Medical, Nursing, Pharmacy, and Dental, with over 45 institutions under its banner.

Driven by visionary leaders, philanthropists, and education lovers, the association has played a major role in the growth of Davangere. Its continued development is guided by the leadership of Dr. Shamanur Shivashankarappa, the Hon. Secretary.

About Our MCA Department

Welcome To Our Department

The Master of Computer Applications (MCA) program, established in 1992, has grown from an intake of 30 to 180 students, meeting the rising demands of the software industry with a modern, industry-aligned curriculum.

The two-year program blends practical learning, emerging technologies, and industry collaboration, supported by experienced faculty with doctoral specializations and strong alumni engagement.

With advanced labs and IT infrastructure, the department has produced over 1300 successful graduates placed in national and multinational companies, shaping globally competitive software professionals.
